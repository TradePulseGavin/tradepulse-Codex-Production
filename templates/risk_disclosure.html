Data and AI limits

Demo or delayed data may be inaccurate, incomplete, or stale. AI and scanner outputs can be wrong or incomplete. Always verify information with your broker, charting platform, exchange, and other trusted sources before making your own decisions.

Paper trade first

TradePulse is designed to encourage paper trading, planning, journaling, and review before real-money decisions. If you are new, start with simulation and small controlled learning steps.
