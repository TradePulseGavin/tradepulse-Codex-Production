Information you provide

TradePulse may store account email, watchlists, journal entries, strategy rules, risk rules, dashboard layouts, and school progress when Supabase persistence is configured.

Payments

Payments are handled by Stripe. TradePulse should not store raw payment card numbers. Stripe may provide subscription status, customer identifiers, and checkout event metadata needed to manage plan access.

AI and screenshots

If AI features are enabled later, user prompts, chart context, journal context, and uploaded screenshot metadata may be sent to configured AI providers to generate research commentary. Screenshot reviews are currently metadata-assisted demo reviews unless real image AI is explicitly connected later.

Data controls

Users can export the TradePulse account/demo data currently available to the app from the Account page. Deletion or correction uses a reviewed request workflow so account data is not removed accidentally.

When Supabase persistence is configured, Row Level Security should be used so users only access their own rows.
